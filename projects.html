Club Laiya Commercial

The amenities inside Club Laiya are a delight to explore. Inside is a different world on its own having only the most premium homes, amenities, and establishments found only in Club Laiya.

Praia

At the edge of Club Laiya is the wonderful Praia Coast that perfectly faces the horizon. The Praia Coast itself houses a number of establishments for you to bask in while feeling every bit of the beach breeze.

Retail

The retail row is a stretch of retail landscape ideal for tourists to avail their needs. It is every entrepreneur’s dream site to grow their local business as it will never fall short of tourist traffic.

Plaza Laiya

There is nothing like unwinding in a quiet resort garden where you can see different types of flora in full bloom. Club Laiya’s resort garden deepens your connection with nature and your love for serene and scenic places.

Kava & Aiya

The Kava and Aiya Boulevard is the center of Club Laiya’s residential - commercial use properties. It is where you can build a highly profitable business such as cafes, retail stores, fashion lines, and many miscellaneous establishments because it is exposed to pedestrian traffic.

Blue Road

Blue Roads are every resident’s exclusive walkway. These roads serve as guides to where the best amenities are in Club Laiya.

Wide Street

Walking in the streets of Club Laiya is family friendly as there is more than enough space to feel the surrounding area and safely explore the whole estate.

Open Spaces

Living in paradise is meant to be breathtaking. Club Laiya’s open space naturally allows you to breathe fresh air while you explore exciting activities and tourist attractions.

Designated Ped & Bike

Everyone is free to explore Club Laiya’s amenities and attractions may it be by foot or by wheels. A designated pedestrian and bike lane makes exploring much easier and safer.

Expansive beachfront

Just like any resort, the beachfront is at the center of attraction. However, Club Laiya boasts an expansive beachfront with a shore that is nourished with clear, teal waters.

Unforgettable Experience

This rising Leisure Tourism Estate is easily the perfect environment for the discerning investor or the imaginative homeowner to build a dream beach place or start a resort business.
